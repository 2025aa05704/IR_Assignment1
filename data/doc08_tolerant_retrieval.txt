Tolerant Retrieval Techniques

Tolerant retrieval helps a search engine answer imperfect queries. Wildcard queries such
as inform* match every term that begins with the prefix inform. Spelling correction uses
edit distance to suggest the closest dictionary term for a misspelled word. A k-gram index
stores character n-grams and speeds up wildcard and spelling correction. Phonetic
correction with the Soundex algorithm matches words that sound alike, such as Robert and Rupert.
