Text Preprocessing Pipeline

Text preprocessing transforms raw text into clean tokens for indexing. The pipeline
begins with tokenization, which splits text into words. Lowercasing normalises the case
of every token. Stop-word removal deletes very common words such as the, is and of.
Stemming reduces words to their root form, while lemmatization maps words to their
dictionary lemma. Proper preprocessing improves both precision and recall of retrieval.
